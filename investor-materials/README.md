# Investor Materials

Central repository for materials to share with investors for Norma - AI Companion for Seniors.

## Folder Structure

- **highlights/** - Key achievements, metrics, and milestones
- **references/** - Testimonials, press coverage, and third-party validation
- **pitch-deck/** - Presentation slides and supporting visuals
- **company-overview/** - One-pagers, executive summaries, and company fact sheets
- **financials/** - Financial projections, funding history, and unit economics
- **market-research/** - Industry data, market size analysis, and competitive landscape
