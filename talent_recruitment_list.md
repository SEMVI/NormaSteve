## Status Legend

- **To Contact** - Initial outreach pending
- **Contacted** - Outreach sent, awaiting response
- **In Discussion** - Active conversations ongoing
- **Interview Scheduled** - Interview process initiated
- **Offer Extended** - Offer made to candidate
- **Hired** - Successfully recruited
- **Declined** - Candidate declined or not a fit
